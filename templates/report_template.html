What the gap actually is

Nearly all of the remaining error sits on the last letter of a word — most often the "ezafe," a short vowel that links two words together (like کتابِ من, "my book"). Deciding whether that link exists at all depends on sentence grammar, not just spelling, which is the hardest sub-problem in this task. Everywhere else in a word, the model is already at — character accuracy.
